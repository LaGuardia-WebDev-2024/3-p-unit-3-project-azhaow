Recipe #1

  • Time: 10 min (5m prep + 5 m cooking; excluding rice
  • 1 serving/plate

Step 1: Cut 2 tomatoes into wedges
Step 2: Crack and beat 3 eggs in a bowl
Step 3: Set the fire to high, add oil, and scramble the eggs. Then set aside
Step 4: Add oil to wok, garlic, and then tomatoes and stir fry for a minute
Step 5: Add some water, sugar, and msg and stir
Step 6: Add the scrambled eggs, mix and cook until the tomatoes are soft, then serve with rice

Recipe #3

  • Time: 75 min (15m prep + 60m chilling)
  • 2 servings

Step 1: Whip together 1 cup of heavy cream with 2 (or 1) tablespoons of sugar.
Step 2: Spread half of the cream on 2 pieces of bread
Step 3: Lay strawberries down diagonally and add the rest of the cream on top
Step 4: Wrap in plastic wrap and mark the diagonal line the strawberries are placed
Step 5: Place in the fridge for an hour
Step 6: Take it out, unwrap, and cut the sandwich in half following the marked diagonal
